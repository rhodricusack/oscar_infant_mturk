Prescreening Form

To take part in this research study your infant must complete an online activity. Since this study aims to assess typical performance, we are specifically looking for infants who were born at term and without congenital abnormalities. In follow-up studies, we will likely expand these inclusion criteria. We would like you to answer the questions below. If your infant is suitable for the study, you will be immediately directed to a page with more information about the study and an online consent form. Please fill out the below form as accurately and as honestly as possible. There are no right or wrong answers. All of your responses are confidential.

 

  • Is your infant between 2 and 12 months old? Yes No
  • Was your infant born preterm (i.e. earlier than 37 weeks into the pregnancy?) Yes No
  • Does your infant have any physical, intellectual or developmental abnormalities (e.g. blindness, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, etc.)? Yes No
  • Was your infant diagnosed with perinatal brain injury? Yes No
